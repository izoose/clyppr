Keep the game.
Drop the voice chat.

Every app you hear gets captured on a separate audio track the moment it's recorded — game, Discord, mic, all split. So in the editor you just untick Discord and the clip keeps the game. No re-recording, no messy audio.

  • Automatic separation — Clyppr detects each app making sound and gives it its own track. Zero setup.
  • Cut or boost, per source — mute a track, or push it to 300% if the game was too quiet.
  • Hear it live — change a track's volume while the clip plays and it updates in real time.
